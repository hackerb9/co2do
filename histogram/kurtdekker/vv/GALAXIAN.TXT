           How to play Galaxian on Virtual Vanessa/Model 100/102

Updated:  November 16, 1994

Keys to use:

      Enter - start new game
      A and Z - move up and down
      Space - fire

Control keys:

      CapsLock - sound on/off
      NumLock - pause the game

Virtual Vanessa-specific keys:

      F10 - reset
      F11 - run faster
      F12 - run slower

Galaxian is a shameless ripoff of the original game.  It is quite playable
even though it is turned sideways on the screen.  Basically blast as many
waves of aliens as you can.  You get bonus ships every 5000 points.
