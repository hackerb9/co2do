	How to play DRIVE/DRIVE2 on Virtual Vanessa/Model 100/102

Updated:  September 6, 1998

NOTE!  YOU MUST LOAD TABLE1.DEC FIRST (select it and press <Enter>)

Keys to use:

      Enter - start new game
      A - Nitro (must hold down gas too!)
      S - steer left
      D - steer right
      H - brake 1
      J - brake 2 (use both for fast stopping)
      K - gas

Control keys:

      CapsLock - sound on/off
      NumLock - pause the game

Virtual Vanessa-specific keys:

      F10 - reset
      F11 - run faster
      F12 - run slower

DRIVE was my attempt at Pole Position.  It's crude but it can be fun
to try and get the 100% mark.  It requires full-throttle driving without
ever crashing for just over one minute.
