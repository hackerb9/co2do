        How to play 'Copter Strike on Virtual Vanessa/Model 100/102

Updated:  November 16, 1994

Keys to Use:

      Enter - start new game
      A and Z - move up and down
      K and L - accelerate left and right (and flip directions)
      Space - fire

Control keys:

      CapsLock - sound on/off
      NumLock - pause the game

Virtual Vanessa-specific keys:

      F10 - reset
      F11 - run faster
      F12 - run slower

In Copter Strike you pilot a well-armed helicopter over a hostile planet and
blow up all the bad guys cruising around on the ground.  Simple enough, huh?

NEVER sit still in Helico.  The little bars at the top of the screen mean
you're sitting still, and you'd better get moving before the tractor beam is
sent down after you.  This tractor beam will kill you.  The tractor beam also
activates if you fly to high up in the sky for too long.

Fly left and right and blow everything up.  DO NOT TOUCH ANYTHING, INCLUDING
THE LITTLE POSTS ON THE GROUND.

You get bonus helicopters at ever-widening intervals:  1000, 2000, 4000,
7000, 11000, 16000, 22000, 29000, etc.
